# Argo Studio + Pixieset Integration for Photographers

Argo Studio is a CRM built exclusively for photographers that integrates directly with Pixieset, the gallery delivery and digital storefront platform used by photographers worldwide. This integration keeps your client data and gallery status in sync so you can manage bookings, deliveries, and storefront sales from a single system for just $17/month.

## What the Pixieset Integration Does

Connecting Argo Studio with Pixieset creates a seamless bridge between your client management and gallery delivery workflows. Client contact details, session information, and gallery activity flow between both platforms automatically, eliminating the manual data entry that slows down your post-production process.

The integration provides synchronized client records across Argo and Pixieset, gallery delivery status tracking within your Argo workflow timeline, storefront purchase notifications in your client activity feed, and automated follow-up sequences triggered by gallery events like first view or download activity.

## Why Photographers Need This

Pixieset has become a favorite among photographers for its clean gallery presentation, built-in storefront, and mobile-friendly client experience. But Pixieset is a gallery platform, not a CRM. Managing inquiries, bookings, contracts, invoices, and client communication still requires a separate system.

The problem arises when these systems do not talk to each other. You end up entering client details twice, manually tracking which galleries have been delivered, and losing visibility into how gallery engagement connects to your overall client relationship. For photographers delivering 50 or more galleries per year, these inefficiencies compound into hours of lost productivity each month.

A connected workflow means that when a new booking enters Argo, the client is ready for gallery creation in Pixieset. When a gallery goes live, Argo knows about it. When a client makes a storefront purchase, that revenue appears in your Argo reports.

## How It Works with Argo Studio

After connecting your accounts, Argo Studio monitors your Pixieset gallery activity and reflects it in your workflow dashboard. Your Argo client timeline shows gallery milestones alongside booking details, communication history, and payment status.

When you upload images and publish a gallery in Pixieset, Argo detects the gallery status change and can automatically trigger your delivery email sequence. The client receives their gallery link through your branded Argo communication templates, keeping the experience consistent with the rest of your client touchpoints.

Pixieset storefront activity also feeds into Argo. If a client purchases digital downloads or prints through your Pixieset store, that transaction appears in your Argo revenue tracking. This gives you per-client revenue totals that include both session fees and product sales, a metric that is impossible to calculate when your systems are disconnected.

Argo can also trigger re-engagement sequences based on Pixieset gallery data. If a gallery is approaching its expiration date and the client has not downloaded their images, Argo sends a reminder. If a client viewed their gallery but did not visit the storefront, Argo can send a curated product recommendation.

## Setup Steps

Connecting Pixieset to Argo Studio takes just a few minutes:

1. Navigate to Settings and then Integrations in your Argo Studio dashboard.
2. Click on Pixieset from the available integration options.
3. Authorize the connection by logging into your Pixieset account.
4. Select your sync scope: all existing galleries or new galleries only.
5. Configure workflow triggers to define which Pixieset events should update your Argo timelines.
6. Set up storefront tracking to pull purchase data into your Argo revenue reports.
7. Send a test gallery link through Argo to confirm everything is connected properly.

Once configured, the integration runs in the background and requires no ongoing maintenance.
