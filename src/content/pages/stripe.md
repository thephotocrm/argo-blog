# Argo Studio + Stripe: Payment Processing for Photographers

Argo Studio is a CRM built exclusively for photographers that integrates with Stripe, the industry-leading payment processor, to handle invoicing, retainer collection, and payment plan management. This integration gives photographers a professional, reliable way to get paid while keeping all financial data connected to client records for just $17/month.

## What the Stripe Integration Does

The Argo Studio and Stripe integration turns your CRM into a complete payment management system. You can create and send invoices, collect retainer deposits, split payments into installments, and track every transaction automatically, all without leaving your Argo dashboard.

Key capabilities include branded invoice creation and delivery through Argo with Stripe payment processing, automatic retainer splits that collect deposits on your defined schedule, real-time payment status tracking within your client workflow timeline, automated payment reminders for outstanding balances, support for credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers through Stripe, and detailed revenue reporting with per-client financial history.

## Why Photographers Need This

Getting paid should be the simplest part of running a photography business, but for many photographers it is one of the most stressful. Chasing payments, manually tracking which clients have paid their retainer, remembering to send invoice reminders, and reconciling income at tax time all consume hours that could be spent shooting or editing.

Photographers also have unique payment structures. A wedding photographer might collect a retainer at booking, a second payment 30 days before the event, and a final balance on the wedding day. Portrait photographers might offer mini-session prepayment or package upgrades after the gallery reveal. These photography-specific payment patterns do not fit neatly into generic invoicing tools.

## How It Works with Argo Studio

When Stripe is connected to Argo Studio, payments become a native part of your client workflow. You create an invoice within Argo, define the payment structure, and the system handles the rest through Stripe's secure processing infrastructure.

For retainer-based bookings, Argo automatically splits the total into your defined payment schedule. The first payment processes immediately when the client signs their contract, and subsequent payments are charged on the dates you specify. If a payment fails, Argo notifies you and initiates a retry sequence.

Payment status updates your workflow in real time. When a retainer clears, Argo can automatically advance the client to the next workflow stage, send a booking confirmation email, or trigger a questionnaire delivery. When a final payment is received, Argo marks the financial portion of the job as complete.

Automated payment reminders go out before scheduled payments, reducing the awkwardness of asking clients for money. Argo handles the follow-up communication using your branded templates, so the client experience stays professional and consistent.

All transaction data syncs to your Argo dashboard, where you can view revenue by time period, by session type, or by individual client. This data is invaluable for understanding your business health and preparing for tax season.

## Setup Steps

Connecting Stripe to Argo Studio takes just a few minutes:

1. Go to Settings and then Integrations in your Argo Studio dashboard.
2. Select Stripe from the integrations list.
3. Click Connect and log in to your existing Stripe account, or create a new Stripe account if you do not have one.
4. Configure your default payment settings including accepted payment methods and currency.
5. Set up your invoice templates with your branding, logo, and standard payment terms.
6. Define your retainer split preferences for common booking types.
7. Enable automated payment reminders and configure the timing of reminder emails.

Once connected, you can create and send invoices from any client record in Argo.
