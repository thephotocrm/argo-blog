# Argo Studio + CloudSpot Integration for Photographers

Argo Studio is a CRM built exclusively for photographers that connects with CloudSpot, the gallery delivery platform known for its branded mobile app experience. By integrating these two platforms, photographers can manage the entire client lifecycle from booking through gallery delivery and beyond, all for just $17/month.

## What the CloudSpot Integration Does

The Argo Studio and CloudSpot integration links your client management workflow directly to your gallery delivery system. Client records stay synchronized, gallery delivery status is tracked automatically, and your Argo workflow progresses based on real-time CloudSpot activity.

Core integration features include automatic client data sync between Argo Studio and CloudSpot, gallery delivery tracking within your Argo workflow dashboard, mobile app engagement metrics for each client gallery, and workflow automations triggered by gallery events such as delivery, first view, and download.

## Why Photographers Need This

CloudSpot stands out in the gallery platform market with its white-label mobile app, giving photographers a branded experience that clients can carry in their pocket. But like all gallery platforms, CloudSpot handles one piece of the photography business puzzle. The booking, communication, invoicing, and follow-up pieces still need a home.

A client downloads their favorites through the CloudSpot app, but you miss the window to suggest wall art. A gallery expires and the client never saw it because your reminder email went to an old address that was updated in your CRM but not in CloudSpot.

These are not hypothetical problems. They are the daily reality for photographers running multiple disconnected platforms, and they cost real revenue and client satisfaction.

## How It Works with Argo Studio

Once connected, Argo Studio becomes your command center while CloudSpot remains your gallery delivery engine. The integration ensures both systems share the same client data and gallery status information.

When a job in Argo reaches the delivery stage, the system checks for the corresponding CloudSpot gallery and can trigger your delivery communication automatically. Gallery viewing data from CloudSpot, including mobile app engagement, feeds back into Argo so you can see how clients interact with their images.

This two-way data flow powers intelligent automations. Argo can send a curated product email when a client favorites specific images in the CloudSpot app. It can trigger a reminder when a gallery approaches expiration. It can flag clients who have not yet downloaded their images so you can follow up personally.

The integration also consolidates your revenue data. Session booking fees from Argo combine with any product sales through CloudSpot, giving you accurate per-client revenue figures in one report.

## Setup Steps

Connecting CloudSpot to Argo Studio is straightforward:

1. In your Argo Studio account, go to Settings and then Integrations.
2. Select CloudSpot from the integrations list.
3. Log in to your CloudSpot account to authorize the connection.
4. Choose whether to sync existing client records or start fresh with new bookings.
5. Map your Argo workflow stages to CloudSpot gallery milestones.
6. Configure notification preferences for gallery events you want tracked in Argo.
7. Test the connection by syncing a current client record and verifying the data appears correctly in both platforms.

The setup process takes approximately five minutes, and the integration runs automatically from that point forward.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does the integration work with CloudSpot's branded mobile app feature?**
Yes. The Argo Studio integration tracks client engagement across both the CloudSpot web gallery and the branded mobile app. When clients view, favorite, or download images through the app, that activity syncs to your Argo client timeline. This gives you visibility into how clients engage with their galleries regardless of the platform they use. All of this is included in Argo's $17/month pricing.

**Q: Can I migrate from HoneyBook or Dubsado to Argo Studio without losing my CloudSpot data?**
Your CloudSpot data remains completely independent of your CRM choice, so switching from HoneyBook or Dubsado to Argo Studio does not affect your galleries. When you connect Argo to CloudSpot, existing gallery data syncs into your new Argo client records. Argo also provides import tools for bringing client data from your previous CRM. Built exclusively for photographers, Argo makes the transition as smooth as possible.

**Q: How does Argo handle CloudSpot gallery expirations?**
Argo Studio monitors gallery expiration dates from CloudSpot and can trigger automated communications before a gallery expires. You can configure the timing of these reminders, such as sending a notification 14 days and then 3 days before expiration. This ensures clients do not miss their download window, reducing support requests and improving the client experience.

**Q: Is there an additional cost for the CloudSpot integration?**
No. The CloudSpot integration is included in your Argo Studio subscription at $17/month. There are no per-connection fees, API charges, or premium tier requirements. You do need an active CloudSpot subscription separately, but the Argo side of the integration carries no extra cost.
