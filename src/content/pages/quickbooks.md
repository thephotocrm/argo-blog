# Argo Studio + QuickBooks Integration for Photographers

Argo Studio is a CRM built exclusively for photographers that integrates with QuickBooks, the leading small business accounting platform, to keep your financial records accurate and up to date. This integration automatically exports invoice data, payment records, and revenue information from Argo to QuickBooks, eliminating manual bookkeeping and making tax preparation straightforward, all for just $17/month.

## What the QuickBooks Integration Does

The connection between Argo Studio and QuickBooks creates an automatic pipeline from your photography bookings to your accounting ledger. When you send an invoice in Argo and a client pays, that transaction flows directly into QuickBooks with the proper categorization and client attribution.

The integration handles automatic export of invoice data from Argo to QuickBooks, payment recording with proper income categorization, client records synchronized between both platforms, revenue reporting that matches between your CRM and your books, expense tracking that connects session costs with session revenue, and simplified year-end tax preparation with properly categorized income.

## How It Works with Argo Studio

After connecting Argo Studio with QuickBooks, your financial workflow becomes largely automatic. When you create an invoice in Argo for a portrait session, wedding booking, or any other photography service, that invoice record syncs to QuickBooks as a receivable. When the client pays through Argo's payment system, QuickBooks records the income with the correct date, amount, and category.

Argo maps your photography service types to QuickBooks income categories. Wedding photography income, portrait session fees, print sales, and travel charges each flow into their appropriate accounts. This categorization means your QuickBooks reports accurately reflect your revenue sources without manual sorting.

For photographers who track expenses per job, Argo can associate session-related costs like second shooter fees, travel expenses, and equipment rentals with the corresponding client record. When these sync to QuickBooks, you get true per-job profitability data.

At tax time, your QuickBooks records are already organized by income category and time period. Your accountant receives clean data that requires minimal additional preparation, which often translates to lower accounting fees and fewer tax surprises.

## Setup Steps

Getting the QuickBooks integration running takes about ten minutes:

1. Log into your Argo Studio account and navigate to Settings, then Integrations.
2. Select QuickBooks from the available integrations.
3. Authorize the connection by logging into your QuickBooks Online account.
4. Map your Argo service types to QuickBooks income categories. Argo suggests common mappings for photography businesses.
5. Choose your sync frequency: real-time for each transaction or daily batch export.
6. Select your starting sync date. You can backfill historical data or start from the current date.
7. Run an initial sync and verify that a recent transaction appears correctly in QuickBooks.

Once configured, the integration requires virtually no ongoing attention. Transactions flow automatically as they occur in Argo.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does the integration work with QuickBooks Online and QuickBooks Desktop?**
The Argo Studio integration currently supports QuickBooks Online, which is the cloud-based version of the platform. QuickBooks Desktop is not directly supported at this time. Since QuickBooks Online is accessible from anywhere and is the version Intuit actively develops, most photographers find it to be the better choice. Argo Studio is available for $17/month with the QuickBooks Online integration included.

**Q: Will historical invoice data from Argo sync to QuickBooks?**
Yes. During setup, you can choose a historical start date for the initial data sync. Argo will export all invoice and payment records from that date forward into QuickBooks. This is particularly useful for photographers switching to Argo Studio from HoneyBook or Dubsado who want a complete financial record in QuickBooks without manual data entry.

**Q: Can I customize how Argo categorizes income in QuickBooks?**
Absolutely. While Argo Studio provides default income category mappings that work well for most photography businesses, you can customize these mappings to match your specific QuickBooks chart of accounts. Built exclusively for photographers, Argo offers photography-specific categories like session fees, retainer deposits, travel fees, print sales, and album revenue as starting points.

**Q: Does the integration handle sales tax for print orders?**
Yes. When you configure sales tax in Argo Studio for applicable products and services, that tax information syncs to QuickBooks along with the transaction data. QuickBooks then includes these amounts in your sales tax reports, making compliance straightforward. Consult your accountant for guidance on which of your photography services and products require sales tax collection in your state.
