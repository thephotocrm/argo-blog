# Argo Studio + Pic-Time Integration for Photographers

Argo Studio is a CRM built exclusively for photographers that connects directly with Pic-Time, one of the most popular gallery delivery platforms among wedding and portrait photographers. This integration bridges the gap between client management and gallery delivery, letting you automate the entire post-shoot workflow from a single dashboard starting at just $17/month.

## What the Pic-Time Integration Does

When you connect Argo Studio with Pic-Time, your client records and gallery delivery become a unified system. Instead of manually copying client emails into Pic-Time, creating galleries from scratch, and then tracking whether clients have viewed their images, Argo handles the connective tissue between your CRM and your gallery platform.

The integration enables automatic gallery link distribution to clients at the right time in your workflow, real-time tracking of gallery delivery status within your Argo dashboard, synchronized client contact information between both platforms, and triggered follow-up sequences based on gallery activity.

## Why Photographers Need This

Wedding photographers juggling 30 or more weddings per season know the pain of post-wedding delivery. You finish editing, upload to Pic-Time, and then need to remember to send the gallery link, follow up if the client hasn't viewed it, and nudge them toward print orders. Each of these steps is a potential dropped ball.

Without an integrated system, photographers rely on spreadsheets, sticky notes, or memory to track which galleries have been delivered and which clients still need follow-ups. That manual tracking is where things fall through the cracks, leading to frustrated clients and missed print sales revenue.

## How It Works with Argo Studio

Once you connect your Pic-Time account to Argo Studio, the workflow becomes straightforward. When you mark a job as "editing complete" in Argo, the system can automatically notify your client that their gallery is ready. Argo pulls delivery confirmation from Pic-Time so you can see at a glance which clients have received and opened their galleries.

Your Argo workflow timelines update automatically based on Pic-Time gallery status. If a client hasn't viewed their gallery within your specified window, Argo triggers a reminder sequence. This keeps your post-delivery communication consistent without requiring you to manually check Pic-Time for each client.

Client contact details stay synchronized between platforms, so when a client updates their email through your Argo client portal, that change reflects in Pic-Time as well. No more sending gallery links to outdated email addresses.

## Setup Steps

Getting started with the Argo Studio and Pic-Time integration takes about five minutes:

1. Log into your Argo Studio account and navigate to Settings, then Integrations.
2. Select Pic-Time from the available integrations list.
3. Authenticate with your Pic-Time credentials when prompted.
4. Map your Argo workflow stages to Pic-Time gallery actions. For example, map "Gallery Ready" in Argo to trigger gallery link delivery.
5. Configure your follow-up preferences, including how long to wait before sending viewing reminders.
6. Test the connection with an existing client record to confirm data flows correctly.

Once connected, all new bookings in Argo will automatically sync with Pic-Time when they reach the gallery stage of your workflow.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need a paid Pic-Time account to use the Argo Studio integration?**
Yes, you need an active Pic-Time subscription to connect with Argo Studio. The integration works with all Pic-Time plan tiers, including their free starter plan for photographers just getting started. Argo Studio itself starts at $17/month with the Pic-Time integration included at no additional cost.

**Q: Will the integration automatically create new galleries in Pic-Time when I book a client in Argo?**
The integration syncs client data and gallery status between platforms, but gallery creation still happens within Pic-Time. When you create a gallery in Pic-Time for a synced client, Argo automatically detects it and updates your workflow timeline. This approach keeps you in control of gallery settings like watermarking, download permissions, and storefront pricing.

**Q: Can I use Argo Studio with Pic-Time if I currently use HoneyBook or Dubsado?**
Many photographers transition from HoneyBook or Dubsado to Argo Studio specifically because of native gallery integrations like the Pic-Time connection. Argo offers import tools to bring your existing client data over, so you do not lose your booking history. The $17/month pricing also means you can run both platforms in parallel during your transition period without a significant cost burden.

**Q: What happens to my data if I disconnect the Pic-Time integration?**
Your client records remain intact in both Argo Studio and Pic-Time. Disconnecting the integration simply stops the automatic syncing between platforms. Gallery links that were already delivered to clients continue to work normally. You can reconnect at any time and the integration will resync your data.
