# Argo Studio + ShootProof Integration for Photographers

Argo Studio is a CRM built exclusively for photographers that integrates with ShootProof, the gallery hosting and print sales platform trusted by thousands of professional photographers. By connecting these two systems, you get a unified view of every client relationship from initial inquiry through final print order, all for just $17/month.

## What the ShootProof Integration Does

The Argo Studio and ShootProof integration connects your client management workflow with your gallery hosting and print fulfillment. Client records in Argo sync with ShootProof so that contact information, session details, and gallery status are always consistent across both platforms.

Key capabilities include automatic client data synchronization between Argo and ShootProof, print sales tracking visible directly in your Argo client timeline, gallery delivery status updates that feed into your workflow automations, and revenue reporting that combines booking income with print sales for a complete financial picture.

## Why Photographers Need This

Photographers who sell prints and products through ShootProof often manage their client relationships in a completely separate system. This creates a disconnection between the booking and delivery sides of the business. A client books through your CRM, you shoot and edit, then you switch to ShootProof for delivery and sales. If a client places a print order or has a question about their gallery, you are toggling between platforms to piece together the full picture.

This fragmentation costs time and creates opportunities for mistakes. Missed follow-ups on gallery expirations, forgotten print order fulfillment, and inconsistent client communication are common symptoms of running disconnected systems.

## How It Works with Argo Studio

When Argo Studio connects with ShootProof, your client timeline in Argo becomes the single source of truth for the entire client journey. After you complete a session and upload images to ShootProof, the gallery status appears in your Argo workflow. You can see when a client first views their gallery, whether they have downloaded images, and what print orders they have placed.

Argo uses this data to power smart automations. For example, you can set up a workflow that sends a personalized print promotion email three days after gallery delivery if the client has viewed their gallery but not yet placed an order. These photography-specific automations would require complex custom setups in general-purpose CRMs.

Revenue from ShootProof print sales syncs back to Argo, giving you complete per-client profitability data. You can see not just the session fee but the total lifetime value of each client, including product sales.

## Setup Steps

Connecting Argo Studio with ShootProof is a quick process:

1. Open your Argo Studio dashboard and go to Settings, then Integrations.
2. Find ShootProof in the integrations directory and click Connect.
3. Enter your ShootProof login credentials to authorize the connection.
4. Choose your sync preferences: whether to sync all existing clients or only new bookings going forward.
5. Map your Argo workflow stages to ShootProof events such as gallery creation, gallery delivery, and gallery expiration.
6. Enable print sales tracking to pull order data into your Argo revenue reports.

The initial sync typically takes a few minutes depending on how many client records you have in ShootProof.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does the integration support ShootProof's print lab partnerships?**
Yes. When a client places a print order through ShootProof using any of their connected print labs, that order data syncs back to Argo Studio. You can see order details, fulfillment status, and revenue in your client timeline. Argo Studio starts at $17/month and includes this print tracking at no additional cost.

**Q: Can I sync my existing ShootProof clients into Argo Studio?**
Absolutely. During setup, you can choose to import your existing ShootProof client database into Argo. The integration matches clients by email address, so if a client already exists in both platforms, their records are merged rather than duplicated. Built exclusively for photographers, Argo handles photography-specific data fields that generic import tools would miss.

**Q: How does this compare to using ShootProof with HoneyBook or Dubsado?**
HoneyBook and Dubsado do not offer native ShootProof integrations. To connect them, you would need to build custom Zapier automations, which adds $20 or more per month in Zapier costs and requires ongoing maintenance. Argo Studio provides a direct ShootProof connection for $17/month total, with no additional third-party tools needed. The integration is also purpose-built for photography workflows, understanding concepts like gallery expiration and print fulfillment natively.

**Q: Will disconnecting the integration delete my ShootProof galleries?**
No. Disconnecting the Argo Studio and ShootProof integration only stops the data sync between platforms. All galleries, client data, and print orders remain untouched in ShootProof. Your Argo client records are also preserved. You can reconnect at any time to resume syncing.
